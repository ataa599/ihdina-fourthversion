Our Services

Transaction Structuring and Product Development

Our team of highly qualified professional has experience of working on complex financing structures including corporate Sukuk. We have the required credentials to provide end-to-end solution for financing under Islamic mode, both short term and long term. Ihdinaa has foot prints on fast growth Fintech landscape, where we have provided product development services to one of the largest AI based digital lending platform.

Specialized Software Services for Islamic Banks

We have developed Aciano Pool Management Software (APMS) in consultation with our technology partner, Aciano Technologies. A dedicated software to facilitate Pool management and profit distribution for the Islamic Financial Institutions. The product has been already installed at a premier foreign bank in Pakistan. APMS has the capability to integrate with various core banking softwares including Temenos and Symbols.

Shariah Manager

is another unique product which enables audit teams to transform manual processes into a framework, thus allowing them more time to focus on process gaps and risk analysis. This language-independent software creates synergy among various audit and compliance clusters.

Learning and Development

Being AAOIFI partner, we have developed high quality training curriculum for the students and practitioners in Islamic finance. Our tailor made modular certification programs, IBCP (Islamic Banking Certification Program) is well appreciated by our partner banks. We have delivered structured trainings to the staff of various banks including AlBaraka, Bank Alhabib and IBP.

Shariah Audit & Compliance

We provide consultancy on risk based Shariah Audit for the institutions involved in providing financial services under Islamic Finance mode including commercial banks, DFIs, Takaful companies and Islamic mutual funds. We are able to carry out comprehensive assignment of documentation review / gap analysis to ensure Shariah compliance.
